The story of the Three Little Pigs

Once uppon a time...

There was three pig brothers, that decided to build their own houses. The first pig, the laziest of them, made it from hay, and it took him very little time.

The second pig, the strongest of them, made it out of wood. And the third brother, who was the smartest, build it out of bricks.

One day, an angry wolf showed up on the first pig's house. Then, he blew once, and destroyed it in no time.

Then, he walked to the second pig's house. He blew, and couldn't destroy it, but he blew stronger, and destroyed it.

Finally, he arrived on the third house, and he tried to destroy it. He blew, and couldn't, and again and again, but all his efforts were in vain.

When he left, the three pig brothers celebrated happily, and lived on the brick house.

The End
